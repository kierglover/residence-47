Your New Neighborhood

Toul Kork is quickly becoming a hub for shopping, eating and luxury living. Located in the north of the city, Toul Kork has a reputation for its large villas and luxury developments.

Take a closer look at some of the amenities in the surrounding environment to get a taste of what Residence47 has to offer you.

  • Brown Coffee Company
  • Residence 47 is situated within walking distance of Brown TK. A extremely popular cafe and bakery with many branches throughout Cambodia.
  • Brown Roastery
    TK
  • Situated a short drive away by car or scooter, Brown Roastery TK is a must-see for the coffee connoisseurs. With experimental brew-types and fresh food, Brown Roastery is the perfect lazy Sunday destination.
  • School in the area
  • Residence47 is located is a few minutes or short walk away from Zaman University and Zaman Primary. There are also a host of international schools in the area and with a school bus to ISPP and Northbridge.
  • TK Avenue
  • Only a short walk away, TK avenue is an open-air mall with a good selection of luxury shops and restaurants. With a Legend cinema, Lucky supermarket and more, this is a popular way to spend your evenings.
  • The Shop
  • In 2009 The Shop opened its doors in Toul Kork. Positioned in a charming single story building that is spacious with a large garden and terrace space for children. This is the perfect weekend escape.
  • The Place Gym
  • The Place is a high end gym fully fitted for any workout regime. With workout classes, personal training and more avaliable, this is the perfect destination for fitness enthusiast. Only a 5 minute drive from Residence47.
